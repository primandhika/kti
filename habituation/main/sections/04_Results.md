**4. Results**

The results demonstrate a gradual increase in primary students'
metacognitive awareness in speaking across the intervention period.
Changes were observed in the consistency and frequency of metacognitive
behaviors rather than in immediate performance gains.

### **Changes in Metacognitive Speaking Behaviors**

Table 2 presents descriptive statistics comparing students'
metacognitive speaking behaviors before and after the intervention.
